Reciclo Game

It's time to recycle!

Sinopsys.

Our goal in this game it's to help "Maipu" City to collect trash and recycle it avoiding obstacles in a infinite and challenging journey.

About our work in this game:

Endless running developed for "Municipalidad de Maipu" (Mendoza, Argentina) with resources provided by them (music, sounds, graphics).

We made the game design and the programming in three weeks
